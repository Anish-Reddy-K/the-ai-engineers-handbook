**What is vibe coding?** AI-assisted development for rapid prototyping and iteration. Trade-offs: speed vs. maintainability.

**Tools**: **Cursor** (AI IDE, Composer, agent mode, `.cursor/rules`). **Claude Code** (CLI, `/init`, `/clear`, `CLAUDE.md`, thinking modes). **GitHub Copilot** (autocomplete, chat). **Others**: Windsurf, Aider, Cline. Comparing features and choosing the right tool.
